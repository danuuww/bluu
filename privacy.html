Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data Bluu collects, how it is used, and how it is stored. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2

Data We Collect

Bluu collects only the data necessary to provide its features. Below is a breakdown of data collected per feature:

General

  • Discord User ID
  • Discord Server ID
  • Channel IDs

Music

  • Track titles played
  • Listening duration
  • Listening statistics

Welcomer

  • Welcome/leave channel
  • Custom messages
  • Custom images

Ticket System

  • Ticket creator and staff
  • Ticket status
  • Form responses

Giveaway

  • Participant entries
  • Prize and duration
  • Required roles

Anonymous Chat

  • Age, gender, bio
  • Chat session data
  • Report history

We do not collect passwords, email addresses, IP addresses, or any data beyond what is listed above.

3

How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate bot features as intended.
  • To maintain server-specific configurations.
  • To track music listening statistics for users.
  • To manage ticket sessions and giveaway entries.
  • To facilitate anonymous chat matchmaking and handle reports.
4

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in a secured MongoDB database.
  • Access to the database is restricted to the bot developer only.
  • We implement reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
5

Data Retention

  • Server configuration data is retained as long as the bot remains in the server. When the bot is removed, the data may be deleted.
  • Anonymous chat data and reports are retained for moderation purposes and may be periodically cleared.
  • Music statistics are retained indefinitely unless a user requests deletion.
6

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we store about you.
  • Request deletion of your data from our database.
  • Opt out of data collection by discontinuing use of the bot.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.

7

Children's Privacy

Bluu does not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13. If we become aware that a user under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to remove that data promptly.

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Bluu after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9

Contact
